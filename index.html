I am Jie Lu, a PhD candidate in Bioengineering with a focus on medical imaging. I have developed a deep understanding of medical imaging processing, segmentation, and quantification. I have a strong background in medical device, such as OCT and MRI. In addition, I have extensive experience in utilizing deep learning techniques for medical image analysis and processing, which has allowed me to develop advanced algorithms for accurate and efficient segmentation, classification, and quantification of medical images. I am now seeking opportunities in the field of medical imaging where I can utilize my skills and expertise to contribute to the development of innovative technologies that help patients. My research focuses on medical imaging quantification and segmentation.
